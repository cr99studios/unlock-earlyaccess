UNLOCK INSTRUCTIONS

UNLOCK is a hybrid webcomic/film web-based platform that will consist of a mixture of graphics and animation videos. Like a comic book, there are panels to showcase the visuals of the narrative. Scroll down the page to view the visual narrative of the hybrid webcomic/film project.

Panels that contains a play button symbol can activate an animation of the scene. Click on the panels with a play button symbol to activate animation.

When you complete a chapter, a button to the next chapter appears. Click on the button to go to the next chapter.

To play the audio in the background, go to the audio player on the bottom left corner when it appears. Then click on the play button.

To access chapter bookmark, click on the bracelet located at the top right corner.

If you are using Google Chrome as your web browser,
refresh the page before going through each chapter.

If website gets glitchy, refresh the page.

Now you've read all of the instructions, it's time to unlock the experience.
